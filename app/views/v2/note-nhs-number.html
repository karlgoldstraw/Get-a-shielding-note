Your NHS number is a 10 digit number that you find on any letter the NHS has sent you. For example, 485 777 3456.
You can also find your NHS number online (link opens in a new window).
Why we are asking this

We need to include this information on your shielding note.

We will use your NHS number to check if you are on the shielded patient list.

It helps identify you if there is more than 1 person with your name at your work.

Your NHS number will also be used to keep your shielding note secure.
