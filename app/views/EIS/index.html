Get a shielding note

Use this service if you work in an area where shielding is in place and you need a note for your employer, so you can get statutory sick pay.

This service is only for people who:

  • are at high risk from coronavirus – you previously received a letter advising you to take extra steps to protect yourself (shielding)
  • live outside of but work inside an area where shielding is in place
  • cannot work from home
  • live in England

You do not need a note if:

  • you can work from home
  • you live and work in the same area where there is a local outbreak – you will get a letter from a GP telling you if you need to take extra steps to protect yourself (shield)
