Use this service if you need a note for your employer, so you can get Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) or Employment and Support Allowance (ESA).

This service is only for people who:

  • are at high risk from coronavirus (clinically extremely vulnerable) – you received a letter advising you to take extra steps to protect yourself (shield)
  • live outside of but work inside an area with a local outbreak (where shielding advice is in place) – visit GOV.UK to check if this applies to you
  • cannot work from home
  • live in England

You do not need a note if:

  • you live and work in the same area – you will get a letter from a GP telling you if you need to take extra steps to protect yourself (shield)
  • you can work from home
