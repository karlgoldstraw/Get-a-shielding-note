You can only get a shielding note if all of these apply:

  • you're at high risk from coronavirus – you received a letter advising you to take extra steps to protect yourself (shield)
  • you work in an area where you’re advised to shield, but do not live there
  • you cannot work from home or outside of an area where you’re advised to shield
  • you live in England

If you live and work in the same area you will get an email or letter telling you if you need to shield.

If you are not sure if this applies to you, speak to your GP surgery, your employer or visit GOV.UK to find out which areas have local restrictions (link opens in a new window).
