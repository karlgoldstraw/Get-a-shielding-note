We will send your shielding note by email if we have your email address on our system.

If we do not we will send it to the address you have provided.

What you need to do next

Once you have your shielding note you can give it to your employer if they ask to see it.

They may check the details you have provided using the Check a shielding note service.

If you have not received your note

If you have not received your shielding note after 4 days check your junk mail folder.

If there’s no email in your junk mail folder speak to your GP surgery.

You can speak to the GP surgery receptionist to explain the situation – they will decide if you need to speak to a GP.
